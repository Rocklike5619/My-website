Welcome, I'm Koroush, and this is my portfolio.

I created this website to showcase my work and challenge myself to see how straightforward building a website could be.

Thanks to the accessibility of AI tools like OpenAI, the process was much smoother. From advice on design and layout to spelling checks and reviews, AI supported almost every step. However, bringing everything together was my task, and it required time, effort, and creativity.

While this site might seem simple, it’s the result of experimenting with various designs, structures, and code. After much trial and error, I landed on a design I’m proud of. It’s basic yet functional and was a rewarding challenge to create.
